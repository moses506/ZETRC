Module 1: Zofunika za ulimi wanzeru pa nyengo
Language: Nyanja

Mwachidule
Gawo ili loyambira limafotokoza kusintha kwa nyengo, kusamalira madzi, ndi kukonzekera famu mwanzeru.

Mfundo zazikulu
1. Yang'anani mmene mvula ikusintha musanasankhe nthawi yobzala.
2. Gwiritsani ntchito njira zosunga madzi monga mulch ndi kulimira msanga.
3. Lembani mwachidule mmene mbewu zilili, tizirombo, ndi chinyezi cha nthaka.
4. Unikani zoopsa za pa famu sabata iliyonse ndikusintha ntchito mwamsanga.

Ntchito yochita
Yendani m'munda umodzi ndi kulemba zoopsa zitatu zomwe nyengo ikubweretsa.
