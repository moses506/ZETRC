Module 2: Kasamalidwe ka nthaka, mbewu, ndi munda
Language: Nyanja

Mwachidule
Gawo ili limaphunzitsa kusamalira nthaka, kusiyana kwa mbewu, ndi ntchito za m'munda zomwe zimathandiza zokolola.

Mfundo zazikulu
1. Tetezani nthaka yapamwamba ndi zotsalira za zomera, manyowa, ndi kuchepetsa kukokoloka.
2. Tsatirani miyeso yabwino yobzala kuti mbewu zikule mofanana.
3. Chotsani namsongole msanga kuti usapikisane ndi mbewu pa chakudya ndi madzi.
4. Unikani kukula kwa mbewu sabata iliyonse ndi kulemba mavuto mwamsanga.

Ntchito yochita
Yesani mzere umodzi wobzala ndikufananitsa mtunda wake ndi muyeso womwe mukufuna.

Ndemanga ya dashboard
Iyi ndi PDF yoyambira yopangidwa kuti igwiritsidwe ntchito mu library ya zilankhulo ziwiri.
