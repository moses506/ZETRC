Module 1: Climate-smart farming essentials
Language: English

Overview
This starter module introduces weather adaptation, water use, and practical farm planning.

Key learning points
1. Observe local rainfall patterns before making seasonal planting decisions.
2. Use simple water-saving practices such as mulching and early weeding.
3. Keep short field notes on crop condition, pests, and soil moisture.
4. Review farm risks every week and adjust activities early.

Practice task
Walk through one field and list three risks caused by changing weather.
