Module 2: Soil, crop, and field management
Language: English

Overview
This starter module covers soil care, crop spacing, and field routines that improve productivity.

Key learning points
1. Protect topsoil with residue cover, manure, and reduced erosion.
2. Follow clear spacing guides for healthier plant stands.
3. Remove weeds early to reduce competition for nutrients and water.
4. Check crop growth weekly and record any problems quickly.

Practice task
Measure one planting row and compare the spacing with your target standard.
